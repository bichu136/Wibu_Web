<p itemprop="description">Voice actresses Aya Suzaki and Asuka Nishi announced at an event in Tokyo on Sunday that their <i>Suzakinishi</i> radio program will get a television anime adaptation starting in July. The <i>Suzakinishi The AanimationN</i> anime will air on Tokyo MX TV, TV Kanazawa, and Sun TV.<br>

(Source: ANN)</p>
