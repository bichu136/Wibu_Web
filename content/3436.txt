<p itemprop="description">Meme, a very cute and strange creature, is this leading character of this series.<br>
One day Meme comes out of a computer and meets Daisuke and Sayaka.<br>
Meme guides the kids throughout his world, where they meet world-famous scientists, inventors and scholars such as Galileo, Pasteur, Kepler, and many more.<br>
This series is not only highly entertaining for children and the entire family but is also very interesting and educational.<br>
