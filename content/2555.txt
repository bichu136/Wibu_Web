<p itemprop="description">After his father's sudden death, Ceddie moves to England as an heir to his grandfather, the Earl of Dorincourt. <br>
<br>
The Earl is a stubborn and selfish old man. Gradually, however, Ceddie's innocent love opens the Earl's heart and changes him into a caring and generous person. <br>
<br>
This is based on "Little Lord Fauntleroy" by F.H.Burnett. <br>
<br>
(Source: AnimeNfo)</p>
