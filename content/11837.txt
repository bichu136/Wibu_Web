<p itemprop="description">The story starts off with a face-off between two rival heroes, ZET and ALPHAS, and then traces their origins - Jin Kanzaki, a young man with the ability to transform into a superhuman being known as ZET, and Kouga Amagi, a young man with a strong sense of justice who uses technology to fight as ALPHAS.<br>
<br>
The fates of these two men and those around them intertwine as they fight to protect mankind and destroy monstrous abominations known as Players.<br>
