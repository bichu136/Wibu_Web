<p itemprop="description">The story begins in a small town, Pennsylvania in 1838. Jeanie MacDowell is a cheerful and beautiful girl with light brown hair. Jeanie enjoys playing the piano and loves taking piano lessons from her mother.<br>
<br>
Steven, a good harmonica player, and Bill, a black boy who is very good at playing the banjo, are great friends of Jeanie's. They enjoy playing music together like a small band.<br>
<br>
However, Jeanie's happy life changes dramatically after her mother suddenly passes away.<br>
<br>
Experiencing many difficulties and learning the importance of life, she decides to devote her life to helping many people suffering from illness.<br>
<br>
This series is a highly emotional love story about the relationship between young Stephen Foster, who later becomes a famous composer and author of children's songs, and young Jeanie MacDowell, who becomes Stephen Foster's wife.</p>
