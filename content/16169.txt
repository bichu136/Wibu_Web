<p itemprop="description">The anime adaptation of the four-panel manga "Ai Mai Mii".<br>
<br>
The story follows girls in a manga club—Ai, Mai, Mii, and Ponoka-senpai—who might be fighting evil invaders threatening Earth, facing off against rivals in tournaments, and dealing with other absurd situations when they are not drawing manga.<br>
