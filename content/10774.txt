<p itemprop="description">Together with the hero of this series, Brian Thompson an ex-reporter, we get to relive the great games of the Football World Cup.<br>
<br>
The story begins with the first selection which took place in Uruguay 1930 until 1994 when it was held in the United States. We also get a depiction of Thompson's life from his youth until now. <br>
<br>
We also see the game brilliantly reborn through spectacular players like Franz Beckenbauer, Müller Gard, Eusebio and the greatest of them all, Pelé.<br>
<br>
(Source: A somewhat rough translation of a summary of the series from the French site "Planéte Jeunesse")</p>
