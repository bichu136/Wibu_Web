<p itemprop="description">Haruka and Sora Kasugano are coming home, to a place filled with memories.<br>
<br>
Having lost their parents in a tragic car accident, the twins resolve to return to the countryside and start life anew at their grandfather's house, the haunt a constant reminder of moments from their past. Greeting them are childhood friends Nao Yorihime and  Akira Amatsume, and newcomer Kazuha Migiwa. It is a warm welcome, symbolic of the days that should come.<br>
<br>
Their peace is merely ephemeral, however, as suppressed emotions, born from vows both newfound and forgotten, start exerting their influence on the twins' new lives. And deep down, a dark secret, only known to them, begins to unshackle.<br>
<br>
Based on the visual novel by Sphere, <i>Yosuga no Sora</i> not only explores the power of lost memories and true love when the bonds of many become intertwined, but also raises the questions of morality and social acceptance.<br>
