<p itemprop="description"><i>Time Travel Shoujo</i> is based on a 1983 book titled <i>Jishaku to Denki no Hatsumei Hakken Monogatari</i> by Japanese educator Kiyonobu Itakura. It is part of the <i>Hatsumei Hakken Monogatari Zenshuu</i> series which describes the story of various scientific discoveries and inventions throughout history. The 1983 book focuses on discoveries related to magnetism and electricity.</p>
